Register Your Account

If you’re a new user, click on the Register button to create your account. Just fill in your details, and you're all set to get started!

Log In to Your Dashboard

Already registered? Simply log in with your credentials to access your personal dashboard, where all your tasks will be stored securely.

Add Your Tasks

After logging in, create new tasks by entering task details such as the title, description, and due date. Organize your tasks easily with categories or priorities.

Set Reminders

Don’t worry about missing deadlines! Set email reminders to receive notifications 2 hours before your tasks are due. Stay ahead of your schedule!

Manage and Update Tasks

Keep your to-do list up-to-date. Edit tasks, mark them as complete, or delete tasks as needed. Your to-do list is fully customizable based on your changing priorities.

Track Your Progress

View all your tasks in one place and track your progress effortlessly. Stay organized and keep your productivity high with a simple, easy-to-use interface

Log Out and Come Back Anytime

Your tasks will be saved securely. You can log out and come back anytime to manage your tasks whenever you need to
